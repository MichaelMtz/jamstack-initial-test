Wachusett Mountain Ski Area

Member Resort since 1981


Wachusett Mountain, located just 60 minutes from Boston, is easily accessible for day trips and winter getaways. The resort is known for its family-oriented atmosphere, with numerous ski lessons, a top ski and snowboard school, and a dedicated "Dev" team for young skiers. Wachusett Mountain boasts a top-notch snowmaking system and grooming services, ensuring excellent skiing conditions year-round. Beyond skiing, Wachusett Mountain offers a variety of outdoor activities, including snowshoeing, hiking, and more, making it a versatile destination for winter enthusiasts.

SnoCountry provides the updated snow reports, weather conditions and mountain information you need for a great day on the snow at Wachusett Mountain.
